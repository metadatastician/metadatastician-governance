= Adoption and Deviations

Upstream: `https://github.com/hyperpolymath/standards` at `2ccc38eaaf8787034ed099e0b72c9ca8b85dc563`.

== Adopted now

* A2ML placement convention: universal `0-AI-MANIFEST.a2ml` at root; ordinary metadata under `.machine_readable/`.
* Descriptile naming for new metadata.
* Separation of authority, profiles, local bindings, and evidence.
* Evidence-status honesty: draft, designed, assumed, tested, audited, and proven are not interchangeable.

== Proposed adoption

The Hyperpolymath constitutional district on `constitution/estate-foundation` is under review. Its authority, assurance, contribution, exception, challenge, and tension principles are proposed for adoption after upstream merge and a pinned-hash update.

== Not automatically applicable

Language/toolchain profiles, release requirements, MAA profiles, and implementation-specific standards apply only when a project binding selects them. Organisational distinction alone does not create a Metadatastician standards fork.

== Deviations

No substantive deviation is authorised in this initial draft. Existing repositories may predate current metadata and terminology conventions; that is migration debt, not an implicit deviation. Each exception requires scope, authority, reason, containment, evidence status, and review date.
