= Contribution Constitution

Status: *Draft for governance review*.

Contribution authority is separate from intellectual-property permission and provenance attestation.

Perimeter 1 — Core:: Named maintainers; high-impact changes require explicit review.
Perimeter 2 — Trusted contributors:: Broader authority earned through public, reviewable criteria.
Perimeter 3 — Community sandbox:: Low-risk proposals and experiments without implied adoption.

Movement between perimeters must be explained and challengeable. No undisclosed founder-only authorship test applies. Any contributor-agreement text based on legal concepts remains labelled *Draft for Legal Review* and is neither legal advice nor an executed agreement.
