= Assurance Profile

Projects MUST label material claims `draft`, `designed`, `assumed`, `tested`, `audited`, or `proven` and identify boundary, assumptions, evidence, tool/version where relevant, limitations, and responsible authority. A repository shape or passing workflow alone is not conformance.

Local bindings record sensitive areas and assurance status. Human review is required for classifications or automated decisions that materially affect a person. Review considers faithful representation, not only formal validity.

Risk acceptance records responsible authority, alternatives, residual risk, expiry/review date, and challenge route. Ordinary rollback is not an MAA claim.
