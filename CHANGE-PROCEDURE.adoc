= Change Procedure

. Open a proposal naming authority, scope, upstream impact, alternatives, evidence status, and affected parties.
. Record any adoption, refinement, non-applicability, or deviation and its exact upstream pin.
. Allow maintainers and affected people a meaningful review/challenge period.
. Obtain approval from the responsible Metadatastician authority; upstream text changes require upstream review, not a local rewrite.
. Record decision, dissent, effective hash/version, migration, and review date.

Emergency containment is temporary and does not itself authorise permanent policy.
