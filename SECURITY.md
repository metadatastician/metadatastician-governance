# Security policy

Do not disclose vulnerabilities, secrets, or personally sensitive evidence in a public issue. Use GitHub's private vulnerability-reporting route for `metadatastician/metadatastician-governance`. If unavailable, contact an organisation owner through the private address published on the Metadatastician organisation profile.

For non-sensitive assurance or policy challenges, open a governance issue. Reports receive human review; no automated classification is final solely because a validator passed.
