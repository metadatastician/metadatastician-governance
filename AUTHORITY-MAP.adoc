= Authority Map

[cols="1,3",options="header"]
|===
|Layer |Authority
|Upstream constitution and standards |Pinned `hyperpolymath/standards` sources; reusable foundation, subject to explicit adoption
|This repository |Local estate profile, adoption choices, deviations, contribution/security routes, and known tensions
|Project binding |Repository-specific applicable profiles, exceptions, local authority, and review date
|Evidence |Tests, audits, proofs, benches, incidents, and STATE records describing what currently holds
|===

The Metadatastician maintainers named by repository ownership approve local policy after review. A lower layer cannot silently contradict a higher applicable layer. Generated output and implementation behaviour cannot create policy authority.
